Welcome to **Cyber O͘-hîm ki-tē** (Cyber Black Bear Base).

This is a digital playground for **Taigi (Taiwanese Hokkien) AI**, tools, and creative experiments. Here you will find web applications, research projects, and resources dedicated to promoting and revitalizing the Taigi language through technology.

Explore our [Apps](/apps.html) and [Projects](/pages/articles.html) to see what we are building.
