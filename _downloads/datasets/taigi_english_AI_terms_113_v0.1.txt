107. 指令階層 (POJ: Chí-lēng kai-chân, 英: Instruction Hierarchy, 日: 指示階層、しじかいそう)

---
Definition: A design approach for AI systems that prioritizes different levels of instructions, ensuring the AI operates according to the most important directives while resisting external interference.

Ex: A tech company implemented the instruction hierarchy method in their customer service system to improve their AI assistant's performance. They divided instructions into three levels: the top level consisted of core principles derived from the company's values, the middle level included standard customer service procedures and response strategies, and the bottom level contained detailed product information and common FAQ answers. When a customer asked a question, the AI assistant would first refer to the top-level instructions to ensure the response aligned with the company's image, then consult the middle-level instructions to determine the appropriate approach, and finally draw specific details from the bottom-level instructions. This method ensured that the AI assistant's responses remained consistent and professional, even when faced with unusual queries. Moreover, if someone attempted to manipulate the AI into producing inappropriate content, the top-level safety instructions would take precedence, preventing the AI from generating unsuitable responses. After implementing this system, customer satisfaction increased significantly, customer service staff efficiency improved, and the company's overall image was enhanced.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

110. LangGraph (POJ: LangGraph, 英: LangGraph, 日: ランググラフ)

定義：LangGraph 是一款開源 ê 框骨，設計來 kā 大型語言 má-to͘h(model) 轉變做濟崁 ê é-jinh(agent/代理人)。伊提供一套工具 kap API，hō͘ 開發者 ē-tàng kā LLM ê 輸出，轉做有狀態 ê 工作流程。LangGraph 利用「定向無循環圖」(DAG) ê 概念，kā 複雜 ê AI 工課拆做較細 ê 步，hō͘ 遮个步數 ē-tàng 互相溝通。Tī 這个 DAG 內底，逐个步數是一個站點(node)，站點 kah 站點 之間 ê 連接，是邊線(edge)，來決定資訊 ê 流動方向。這款設計，hō͘ AI 系統 ē-tàng 處理 khah 長 ê 對話、多步 ê 推理，iah-sī 愛記牢進前資訊 ê 工課。LangGraph mā 提供工具，thang 監控 kap tī-pák(debug) 遮个工作流程，hō͘ 開發者 khah 輕可來創造複雜 ê AI 應用程式。

例 1：一間金融科技公司用 LangGraph 來開發一套巧 ê 股票投資系統。In kā 投資決策流程拆做幾若个步，一步用一个站點(node) 代表：ùi 新聞網站 kah 社群媒體--ni̍h 揣著相關 ê 資訊、分析公司財報 kah 市場趨勢、預測股票價數、kap 決定敢愛買賣股票。遮个站點(node) 透過 LangGraph ê DAG 結構，用墘線(edge)連--起來。系統會記牢進前 ê 投資決策 kah 結果，hō͘ 伊 ē-tàng ùi 經驗--ni̍h 學習，koh ē-tàng 應付複雜 ê 市場狀況，來 kā 投資報酬上大化。

例 2：為著欲設計一套 ē-tàng 自動寫新聞稿 ê 系統，一間新聞媒體公司 kā LangGraph 用--落來。In kā 新聞稿 ê 寫作過程，拆做幾若步，做 tī LangGraph 內底，成做一个一个 ê 站點(node)：ùi 各款新聞來源--ni̍h，kā 相關 ê 資訊 khioh(抾)--出來、kā 遮个資訊整理 kah 概要、生成新聞稿 ê 草稿、koh kā 草稿修改 kah 編輯。遮个站點(node) 透過 LangGraph ê DAG 結構，用墘線(edge)連--起來。系統 ē-tàng ùi 記者寫 ê 新聞稿，自動生成無仝語言 ê 新聞報導，koh ē-tàng kā 新聞發布去無仝款 ê 平台，hō͘ 新聞傳播 kah 較闊。
---
Definition: LangGraph is an open-source framework designed to transform large language models (LLMs) into multi-step agents. It provides a set of tools and APIs that allow developers to convert LLM outputs into stateful workflows. LangGraph utilizes the concept of a Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) to break down complex AI tasks into smaller steps that can communicate with each other. In this DAG, each step is represented as a node, and the connections between nodes are edges that determine the flow of information. This design enables AI systems to handle longer conversations, multi-step reasoning, or tasks that require remembering previous information. LangGraph also provides tools for monitoring and debugging these workflows, making it easier for developers to create complex AI applications.

Ex 1: A fintech company used LangGraph to develop a sophisticated stock investment system. They broke down the investment decision-making process into several steps, each represented as a node: gathering relevant information from news websites and social media, analyzing company financial reports and market trends, predicting stock prices, and deciding whether to buy or sell stocks. These nodes were connected through edges in LangGraph's DAG structure. The system remembered previous investment decisions and outcomes, allowing it to learn from experience and adapt to complex market conditions to maximize investment returns.

Ex 2: To design a system capable of automatically generating press releases, a news media company utilized LangGraph. They divided the press release writing process into several steps, represented as nodes within LangGraph: extracting relevant information from various news sources, summarizing and organizing this information, generating a draft press release, and revising and editing the draft. These nodes were connected through edges in LangGraph's DAG structure. The system can automatically generate news reports in different languages from journalists' drafts and publish them to various platforms, expanding the reach of their news coverage.

111. 人類相kēng(楗)強化 (POJ: Jîn-lūi sio-kēng kiông-hòa, Human-in-the-Loop Augmentation, 日: 人間参加型オーグメンテーション、にんげんさんかがたオーグメンテーション)

定義：kā AI 工具 hām 專業 ê 工課流程 tàu-tah(鬥搭)--起來，目標是欲增強人類 ê 專業能力，m̄ 是欲 kā 取代--去。這个做法強調講，人類專家 kap AI 系統互相合作，chham人類 ê 判斷、創意、kah 經驗相食穿，chham AI 快速處理資料、認捌模式、koh 生成(seng-sêng)洞見 ê 才調，按呢來 kā 工課 ê 品質、效率、kah 成果提升。
Definition: The integration of AI tools into professional workflows to enhance human expertise, rather than replace it. This approach emphasizes collaboration between human experts and AI systems, combining human judgment, creativity, and experience with AI's ability to rapidly process data, recognize patterns, and generate insights, thereby improving the quality, efficiency, and outcomes of work.

這馬 ê 挑戰(2024)：欲 án-chóaⁿ 有效利用 AI 工具，koh kā 伊 ê 效益發揮 kah 上大，hō͘ 伊 贏過 AI 抑是 人類單獨做工課 ê 成果，猶有困難 。一个主要 ê 挑戰，就是欲 án-chóaⁿ 設計會用--得 kā 人類 ê 專業 kap AI ê 能力無縫相kap(佮) ê 系統 kah 介面，而且 hō͘ 專業人員感覺自然 koh 直覺，m̄-bián(毋免)變做 AI 專家才有法度來用。
Current Challenge(2024): Effectively leveraging AI tools and maximizing their benefits to surpass the capabilities of either AI or humans alone remains a challenge. One major challenge lies in designing systems and interfaces that seamlessly integrate human expertise with AI capabilities and feel natural and intuitive to professionals, without requiring them to become AI experts.

2025 年 ê 期待：開發改良 ê 系統 kap 介面，hō͘ 專業人員 khah 簡單利用 AI，而且 m̄ 免有 AI 專門智識。這包括較直覺 ê用者介面、ē-hiáu(會曉) 解釋家己按怎做決定 ê AI 系統、koh 有 kā 人類 ê 回饋 kap 氣口同齊考慮 ê 學習演算法。
2025 Expectation: The development of improved systems and interfaces that make it easier for professionals to utilize AI without requiring specialized AI knowledge. This includes more intuitive user interfaces, explainable AI systems that can articulate their decision-making processes, and learning algorithms that incorporate human feedback and preferences.

例 1 (台語-英語翻譯)：�一間語言科技公司開發一个 AI 接力 ê 台語-英語翻譯系統，目標是欲 kā 台語翻譯 ê 品質提升，特別是 tī 複雜 iah-sī 罕得見 ê 詞彙 kap 句型這tah-ūi。這个系統用一个大型 ê 台語-英語平行語料庫來訓練，而且用人類相kēng(楗)強化 ê 步數，hō͘ 專業 ê 台語翻譯人員 ē-tàng 隨時 kā AI 生成(seng-sêng) ê 翻譯校對、修改、kap 認可。AI 系統會 ùi 人類 ê 回饋--ni̍h 學習，勻勻仔 kā 家己 ê 翻譯能力改善。閣有，這个系統 mā 提供一个用者友善 ê 介面，hō͘ 翻譯人員 ē-tàng 緊緊揣著相關 ê 詞彙、例句、kap 翻譯準則，hō͘ in ê 工課 khah 順序。這款人類 kap AI 合作 ê 模式，m̄-nā hō͘ 台語翻譯 ê 效率提升，mā hō͘ 翻譯 ê 品質 khah 穩定、khah 正確，特別是 tī 專業領域，親像法律、醫學、iah-sī 技術文件。這个例 kā 咱展現講，人類相kēng(楗)強化 ē-tàng 克服純粹 AI 系統 ê 限制，而且 kā 人類 ê 專業 kap AI ê 處理能力鬥搭--起來，hō͘ 翻譯工課閣較完善。
Example 1 (Taigi-English Translation):�A language technology company is developing an AI-assisted Taigi-English translation system aimed at improving the quality of Taigi translations, particularly for complex or rare vocabulary and sentence structures. The system is trained on a large Taigi-English parallel corpus and employs a human-in-the-loop augmentation approach, allowing professional Taigi translators to review, edit, and validate the AI-generated translations. The AI system learns from the human feedback, gradually improving its translation capabilities. Additionally, the system provides a user-friendly interface that allows translators to quickly access relevant terminology, example sentences, and translation guidelines, streamlining their workflow. This collaborative approach between humans and AI not only enhances the efficiency of Taigi translation but also ensures higher consistency and accuracy, especially in specialized domains such as legal, medical, or technical documents. This example demonstrates how human-in-the-loop augmentation can overcome the limitations of purely AI-driven systems and combine human expertise with AI's processing power to achieve superior translation outcomes.

例 2 (教育): 一个線上學習平台開發一个 AI 接力 ê 寫作輔導系統，目標是欲 kā 學生 ê 寫作技巧提升，而且 hō͘ 老師 khah 輕鬆。這个系統用自然語言處理技術，ē-tàng 分析學生 ê 作文，koh 提供文法、風格、kap 結構方面 ê 建議。M̄-koh，m̄ 是 kā 錯誤自動改--過來，系統會 kā 有問題 ê 所在標--出來，而且 hō͘ 學生提示，hō͘ in 家己思考 án-chóaⁿ 改。老師 mā ē-tàng 參與這个過程，提供 koh-khah 個人化 ê 指導 kap 回饋。這款人類相kēng(楗)強化 ê 步數，m̄-nā hō͘ 學生 ê 寫作能力進步，mā 培養 in ê 批判思考 kap 自我編修 ê 能力。一个初步 ê 研究顯示，用這个系統 ê 學生，in ê 作文分數有顯明 ê 提升，而且 in 對寫作課 ê 參與度 kah 自信嘛有增加。這个例 kā 咱展現講，人類相kēng(楗)強化 ē-tàng 創造 koh-khah 有效 koh 有互動性 ê 學習環境，而且 kā 老師 kap 學生 ê 潛力 lóng tháu-pàng(敨放)--出來。
Example 2 (Education): An online learning platform developed an AI-assisted writing tutoring system to improve students' writing skills and reduce teachers' workload. The system uses natural language processing techniques to analyze students' essays and provide suggestions on grammar, style, and structure. However, instead of automatically correcting errors, the system highlights potential issues and prompts students to think about how to revise their work. Teachers can also participate in the process, offering more personalized guidance and feedback. This human-in-the-loop augmentation approach not only enhances students' writing proficiency but also fosters their critical thinking and self-editing abilities. A preliminary study showed that students using this system significantly improved their essay scores and demonstrated increased engagement and confidence in writing classes. This example illustrates how human-in-the-loop augmentation can create more effective and interactive learning environments, unlocking the potential of both teachers and students.

112. AI 數位懸低坎(POJ: AI sò͘-ūi koân-kē-khám, 英: AI Digital Gap, 日: AI デジタル格差、エーアイ デジタルかくさ)

定義: AI 技術 ê 發展、應用、kap 利益分配 tī 無仝地區、社群、iah-sī 族群之間 ê 無平等現象。這款落差會 hō͘ 本來就 tī 社會、經濟、iah-sī 教育方面 ê 無平等 koh-khah 嚴重。
Definition: The unequal development, adoption, and distribution of benefits of AI technologies across different regions, communities, or demographic groups. This gap can exacerbate existing social, economic, or educational inequalities.

例 1 (低資源語言):相像台語這款低資源語言，因為 tī 網路頂懸 ê 數位內容 kah 訓練資料相對少，leh 開發 AI 應用 ê 時陣，定定 hông 放捒。這造成一个惡性循環：因為較無 AI 工具支援台語，台語 ê 數位內容 to̍h 較袂成長，煞 hō͘ 台語 tī AI 時代愈來愈邊緣化。譬論講，雖然主流 ê 語音辨識系統 ē-tàng 支援濟濟款語言，毋過其中台語 ê 辨識正確率 iáu-koh 有一段坎差，因為訓練資料無夠額。這 m̄-nā 限制台語使用者享受 AI 技術 ê 好處，mā 對台語 ê 保存 kah 推廣造成威脅。這个例顯示講，AI 數位懸低坎差會 hō͘ 低資源語言 tī 數位時代 ê 弱勢地位 koh-khah 嚴重，而且 hō͘  語言多樣性 ê 保護挑戰。
Example (Low-Resource Language):Low-resource languages like Taigi are often left behind in AI development due to the relative scarcity of digital content and training data available online. This creates a vicious cycle where the lack of AI tools for Taigi hinders the growth of its digital presence, further marginalizing the language in the AI era. For instance, while mainstream speech recognition systems can support numerous languages, the accuracy for Taigi remains noticeably lower due to insufficient training data. This not only limits Taigi speakers' access to the benefits of AI technologies but also poses a threat to the preservation and promotion of the language. This example illustrates how the AI digital gap can exacerbate the disadvantaged position of low-resource languages in the digital age and pose challenges to linguistic diversity.

例 2 (教育機會):
AI 接力 ê 個人化學習平台，有法度 hō͘ 學生有 koh-khah 有效 ê 學習體驗，毋過 in ê 使用，tī 無仝地區 ê 學校有誠大 ê 差別。有錢 ê 學校 ē-tàng 買較好 ê 軟硬體設備，而且 老師 mā 有較濟機會接受相關訓練，hō͘ in ê 學生有較濟機會接觸著 AI 教育工具。相對來講，散赤地區 ê 學校 to̍h 欠缺資源，hō͘ 學生 tī 數位時代 ê 競爭力受著限制。這款 AI 教育懸低坎差，會 kā 社會階層流動 ê 阻礙加深，而且 hō͘ 弱勢學生較oh(僫)得著成功 ê 機會。
Example 2 (Educational Opportunities):
AI-powered personalized learning platforms can enhance learning experiences for students, but their adoption varies significantly across different school districts. Wealthier schools can afford better software and hardware, and their teachers often have more opportunities to receive training on these tools, providing their students with greater access to AI educational resources. In contrast, under-resourced schools in disadvantaged areas lack access to these technologies, limiting their students' competitiveness in the digital age. This AI educational gap can hinder social mobility and make it harder for students from disadvantaged backgrounds to succeed.

API / 應用程式介面 (POJ: API、Èng-iōng-thêng-sek kài-bīn, 英: Application Programming Interface, 日: API、エーピーアイ)

定義：一款軟體中介物，hō͘ 兩个應用程式 ē-tàng 互相溝通。伊是一組定義 kap 協定，hō͘ 開發者 ē-tàng 建立會當 kap 其他軟體系統整合 ê 應用程式。簡單來講，API 親像一个服務生，tī 用者 kap 系統當中走傱， kā 用者 ê 需求傳達 hō͘ 系統知，koh kā 系統 ê 回應傳--轉去 hō͘ 用者。

例：你 leh 用手機仔 App 叫外送 ê 時，App 會用 API kap 餐廳 ê 系統溝通，傳送你 ê 訂單資訊，koh kā 你回報餐點 ê 製作狀況。地圖 App mā 是用 API ùi 外部服務提著地圖資料 kap 路線資訊，hō͘ 你 ē-tàng 看著地圖、揣路、kap 導航。社群媒體 App 用 API hō͘ 你 ē-tàng ùi 無仝平台分享內容，親像 kā Instagram ê 相片分享去 Facebook。總講--一句，API hō͘ 軟體系統之間 ē-tàng 互相合作，hō͘ 用者 koh-khah 方便、功能 koh-khah 豐沛 ê 使用體驗。
