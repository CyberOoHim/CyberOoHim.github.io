About {{ site.title }}

Welcome to {{ site.title }}, a Taigi AI playground! This site is dedicated to exploring and showcasing the possibilities of Artificial Intelligence in the context of the Taigi (Taiwanese) language.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop and promote Taigi-language AI tools, resources, and research. We believe that preserving and revitalizing languages like Taigi is crucial, and AI can play a significant role in this effort.

What We Do

  • Develop Taigi language processing tools (e.g., the TL to POJ converter).
  • Create and curate Taigi language datasets.
  • Conduct research on Taigi AI.
  • Share our findings and resources with the community.
  • Promote the use of Taigi in the digital age.
