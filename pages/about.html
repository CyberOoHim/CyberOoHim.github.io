Welcome to {{ site.title }}, a playground for exploring Artificial Intelligence (AI) in the context of the Taigi (Taiwanese) language. We focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP) to develop tools, resources, and research to promote Taigi in the digital age.

Our Mission

To develop and promote Taigi-language AI to help preserve and revitalize the language.

What We Do

  • Develop Taigi language processing tools (e.g., TL-POJ converter - coming soon!).
  • Create and curate open-source Taigi language datasets.
  • Conduct research on Taigi AI, including speech recognition and machine translation.
  • Share our code, datasets, and findings on GitHub.
